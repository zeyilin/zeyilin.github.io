SplitTab — Privacy Policy

Effective March 12, 2026

SplitTab is a bill-splitting app that runs entirely on your iPhone. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. This policy explains what that means in detail.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect any personal information whatsoever. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or account information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Location data
  • Photos or receipt images (processed entirely on your device)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Advertising identifiers

What's Stored on Your Device

SplitTab stores your receipts, contacts, and preferences locally on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device through any mechanism we control. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Crash diagnostics are handled by Apple's MetricKit framework, subject to your iOS privacy settings.

Venmo

SplitTab can open the Venmo app to help you send payment requests. We simply open Venmo with a pre-filled amount — no data is sent to Venmo's servers by SplitTab. All payment processing is handled by Venmo under their own privacy policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

SplitTab is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.
